## Overview

StepWise ("we", "us", "our") operates a Socratic-style math tutoring system for students in grades 4-9. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

- **Problem submissions**: Math problems entered by students

### Parent/Guardian Information

- **Email address**: Used to send learning reports and weekly digests

### Technical Information

- **Device identifiers**: Anonymous user IDs stored in browser localStorage

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

StepWise is designed for use by children under 13 with parental involvement.

### Our COPPA Commitments:

1. **No direct collection from children**: We do not require children to provide personal information
2. **Parent email for reports**: Session reports are sent to the parent/guardian email provided during setup
3. **No behavioral advertising**: We do not serve targeted ads to children
4. **No social features**: No chat, messaging, or public profiles
5. **Parental control**: Parents can request data deletion at any time

### What We Do NOT Collect from Students:

- Names or personal identifiers
- Photos or videos
- Location data
- Contact information
- Social media accounts

## Email Communications
